Dear Fighters, Coaches and Friends

I would like to invite you all to Yokso Dutch Open 2012 on Saturday 28th April.

This year you have the opportunity to stay a bit longer and enjoy Queensday in Amsterdam together with over 1 million people. We would be honoured to have you here as guests in Amsterdam. Last year the Grand Champion was Sean McCartney who beat Timmy Sarantoudis in the Finals. Let's see what this year will bring.

I look forward to welcoming you at the Yokoso Dutch Open 2012, USH!
